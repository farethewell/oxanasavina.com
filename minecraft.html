Responsive web design

Minecraft mini-site is a collaboration between MetroTunnel Melbourne and Minecraft. It creates an opportunity for kids to play and be a part of a new metro station design. The goal was to be on brand and make something fun to use. I came up with the idea to use a full-screen one-page website with easy information to digest, imagery to help understand it more easily, and some subtle pixel animation.
