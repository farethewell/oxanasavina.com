UKO Property Management

UKO is a leading Australian co-living property management business. The vision for the app was to create a new breed of 'living space' that is more connected, friendly, helpful, and supportive. Where everything one needs will be in one place - bills, internet, weekly cleaning, basic supplies and more. This app provides a platform to communicate, manage and customize a community-led living experience.
